The Santiago Bernabu is once again set to be the stage for a clash of footballing titans, as Real Madrid welcomes Manchester City in a game that feels less like a group stage fixture and more like a final itself.

For the Merengue, the pressure is immense; after a frustrating 0-2 home defeat in their last LaLiga outing, theyll be desperate to deliver a statement performance for their fans against a true European elite. With only two games remaining after this massive fixture, securing a win at the Bernabu is crucial to their hopes of ensuring a smooth passage into the higher echelons of the League Phase rankings.
